# SoundEffectPlay

Plays a sound effect when the action starts. A sound effect is usually a
relative short sound sample that is meant to hint the user of something
happening.

-   **path**: The path of the sound file to play.
-   **volume**: Initial volume of the sound
-   **speed**: Initial speed of the sound.
-   **pan**: The stereo panning, -1 indicates the left side, 0 is the
    center and 1 is the right side.
